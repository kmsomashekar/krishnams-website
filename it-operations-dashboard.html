Executive Summary

This executive dashboard consolidates IT operational performance into a single leadership view, enabling decision-makers to monitor infrastructure availability, service delivery, IT assets, cybersecurity indicators and operational trends.

It has been designed to support executive reviews, operational governance and strategic planning through clear, data-driven reporting.
